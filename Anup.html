Technical Explanations

1. What is Bootloop?

A bootloop occurs when a device is unable to complete its startup process and continuously restarts. Common causes
include corrupted system files, failed software updates, or incompatible apps.

Solutions:

  • Restart in safe mode
  • Clear cache/data
  • Factory reset
  • Reinstall OS

2. What is a Local Area Network (LAN)?

A LAN is a network that connects computers within a limited area such as a home, school, or office.
